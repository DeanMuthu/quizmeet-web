Last Updated: June 2026

Quick Summary

  • Your data is hosted within the European Union whenever possible.
  • We never sell your personal data.
  • You may request deletion or export of your data at any time.
  • You remain in control of your privacy settings and consent choices.

1. Data Controller

QuizMeet is operated by its publisher.
Contact: support@quizmeet.fr

2. Information We Collect

Account Information

  • Email address
  • Username

  • Interests and preferences

Optional Information

Usage Data

  • Matches

Payment Information

Payments are processed by Apple, Google Play or Stripe. QuizMeet never stores payment card details.

  • Provide the service
  • Create matches

  • Prevent abuse and fraud
  • Improve the platform
  • Send notifications

4. Legal Basis (GDPR)

  • Performance of a contract

  • Legitimate interests

5. Data Retention

Account data is retained while the account remains active.

  • Deleted accounts remain recoverable for 30 days.
  • Permanent deletion occurs after 30 days.
  • Certain legal records may be retained where required by law.

6. Third-Party Services

QuizMeet may use:

7. International Transfers

When data is transferred outside the European Union, appropriate safeguards and legal protections are applied.

8. User Rights

Under GDPR, users may:

  • Withdraw consent

Requests can be sent to:
support@quizmeet.fr
